### Decimals vs. Binaries: the fundamental difference between humans and AI

<i>question</i>: Why is the decimal system so ingrained in humans, while AI (aka machines) defaults to binary?

<i>short answer</i>: Our numbering system emerged from counting using our fingers - ten in total. AI, however, lacks fingers. Therefore, despite the advancements in AI and computing, we remain fundamentally different species - measured by the number of fingers.
