### Where is my bunny?

In the second study in <i>[1]</i>, <i>Kosinski</i> embarks on a unique experiment around cat detection, and I think it would be more interesting to take this study one step further to say, seeking a bunny.

#### Why?

Well, bunnies are champions of hide-and-seek. Usually, they have their favored haunts, but sometimes they take a detour and pop up elsewhere. This tests the observation skills of both humans and AI, pushing us to maintain a comprehensive history of bunny sightings and draw on it to make educated guesses. For instance, by considering the prevailing circumstances, we can better hypothesize about the bunny's current hideout.
