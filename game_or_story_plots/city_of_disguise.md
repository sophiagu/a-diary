### City of Disguise

In _City of Disguise_, participants are thrown into a metropolis, a melting pot of both humans and robots. However, their creature-ships are not immediately distinguishable w/o gathering clues deeper into the game. Each character comes with a unique ability, though it's not automatically revealed. Players can strategically choose to either disclose their identities or skills or hold them in secrecy.

As the game begins, alliances are but a concept. Players are free to form pacts with others, a decision that could come with the choice to unveil their hidden abilities. This disclosure can bolster team strategies and tactics, promoting collaborative gameplay in the early stages. Yet remember: in the end, only one survivor will reign.

The backdrop for this high-stakes survival game is an expansive cityscape, replete with myriad buildings for exploration and interaction. Players may choose to blend into the cityscape by assuming regular day jobs, entangling themselves further into the city's fabric and intensifying the bewilderment of their adversaries. Be aware, one's seemingly benign coworker might well be a camouflaged competitor. The challenge lies in artfully eliminating such threats while preserving the cover of a conscientious employee, all before unmasking and eliminating all opponents within that space. Thus, it is a game not just of survival but of outsmarting one's opponents, forcing them to unravel the truth hidden in plain sight.

This game combines elements of mystery, tactic, and survival. The choices one makes, alliances one forms, and secrets one reveals will ultimately determine one's fate in the game.

And, who will be the last one standing?
